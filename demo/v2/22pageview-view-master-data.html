Case Details:
Subject:

Directive pageview - view master data

Business Logic:
  • Pageview auto loads the department records on start
  • Single click/touch on record will trigger the CustomPointedToRecord() function
  • Double click/select on record will trigger the CustomSelectedToRecord() function
  • Maximum data default as 10 records each page
Procedures:
  1. Double click on the record to select it
  2. or Single click on the record and click [Select] button to select it
  3. Click the arrow buttons to navigate to pages
