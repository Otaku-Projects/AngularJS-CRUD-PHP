List and Delete Department

Case Details:
Subject:
Directive pageview, entry - list, delete department record
Business Logic:
  • Select department record on start
  • Shows department records if exits
  • Select to the department record on pageview
  • Entry display the selected record
  • Refresh the pageview after deleted the record
Procedures:
  1. Single click/touch on the record to point it
  2. or Double click on the record to select it
  3. The record will shows on entry
  4. Preview/confirm that record
  5. Click the [Delete] button
