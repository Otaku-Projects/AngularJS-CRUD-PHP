Select Record in Editbox

Case Details:
Subject:

Directive editbox - select reference key on entry

Business Logic:
  • Editbox's pageview auto loads the department records on start
  • Single click/touch on record will trigger the CustomPointedToRecord() function
  • Double click/select on record will trigger the CustomSelectedToRecord() function
Procedures:
  1. Click on the button
  2. Double click on the record to select it
  3. or Single click on the record and click [Select] button to select it
  4. Click on the button
  5. Single click/touch on the record to point it
  6. Click outside pageview or [back] button to close the pageview
