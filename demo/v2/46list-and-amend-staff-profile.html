List and Amend Staff Profile

Case Details:
Subject:

Directive entry, editbox - list, create staff record

Business Logic:
  • Mandatory Field(s): Staff ID
  • Editbox auto loads the department records
  • Staff records auto loads in the pageview
  • The selected record will shows on entry
  • Refresh the pageview after deleted the record
Procedures:
  1. Double click/point and select on the record to select it
  2. The record will shows on entry
  3. Update the staff record
  4. Click the [Amend] button
